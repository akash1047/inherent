# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We expect contributors to keep collaboration direct, respectful, and technically grounded.

Examples of acceptable behavior:

- focusing discussion on facts, code, and reproducible behavior
- giving and receiving technical feedback in good faith
- documenting assumptions and trade-offs clearly
- helping keep the project accessible to new contributors

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- trolling, baiting, or deliberately disruptive conduct
- publishing private information without permission
- misrepresenting project status, ownership, or contributor intent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributions that violate this policy.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, contact the maintainers through the channel listed in [SUPPORT.md](https://github.com/inherent-prime/inherent/blob/main/SUPPORT.md). For security issues, use [SECURITY.md](https://github.com/inherent-prime/inherent/blob/main/SECURITY.md) instead of public discussion.
